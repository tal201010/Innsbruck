About the city

Innsbruck is one of the best ski resorts in Austria.

The city itself is located in the Inn River valley, at the very foot of the Eastern Alps. Therefore, it is almost impossible to find a place in this city without seeing the mountains.

Innsbruck in numbers:

  • Population: just over 130,000 people
  • Time zone: UTC+1 in winter and UTC+2 in summer
  • Ski areas: 9 pieces, all connected by transport
  • Height difference: 860 meters — 3100 meters
  • Season duration: from 12 months (December) to 4 months (April) inclusive
  • The age of visitors to all zones: from 2 years and older, without restrictions.
